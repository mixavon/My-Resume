Introduction:

I’m Swedish but grew up in Taiwan, where I’ve lived for over 30 years. I’m fluent in English, Chinese, Swedish, and Korean. With 7 years of online teaching experience and 3 years in classroom settings, I also hold two separate 120-hour TESOL certificates.

Living as a foreigner in Taiwan has given me incredible opportunities to immerse myself in the local culture and connect with students of all ages, from 3 to 65. Teaching is my passion, and I genuinely enjoy helping students learn and grow, no matter their age or background.
